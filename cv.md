### Research Interest

My main research interests are 3D Vision, Vision-Language Models (VLMs), and Image Generation. The main problems I aim to address are as follows:

-   Images generated solely from text prompts often fail to match the desired structure or visual details. I am interested in improving the quality and controllability of generated images by leveraging both an initial image draft and textual input, enabling more precise and reliable image generation.

-   While existing VLMs generate responses based on static images and text, I am particularly interested in developing VLMs that can reason over real-time video streams. My goal is to build models that understand ongoing visual content and answer questions based on the temporal context and dynamics of live video.
